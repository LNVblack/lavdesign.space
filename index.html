Electronics Hardware / Embedded Firmware Engineer.

About me: I am a freelance engineer with more than 22 years experience in various areas of designing and developing analog and digital electronics. I am a full-stack developer, I do electronics hardware design from concept to finished product. All development stages from research and analysis to circuit design, PCB design (2-8 signal layers), 3D PCB modeling, specifications, firmware programming C/C++, debugging, etc. And also prototyping and preparing the product for commercialization. I work in professional soft: Altium Designer, Solid Works, Multisim, MS Visual Studio, Microchip Mplab, AutoCAD.

In analog electronics, my main experience is power supplies for lighting applications: LED-drivers, fluorescent /halogen lamp ballast and etc. In my projects used various solutions on buck/boost- convertors, fly-back, passive and active PFC (power factor corrector). Based on both discrete components and specialized IC such as: International Rectifier IR2166, Infinion ICB1Fl02G, Texas Instrument UCC28810, Supertex HV9961 and etc.

In recent years, I have focused on a digital electronics. In this area my main experience is creating intelligent control systems: access control system (based on RFID), fire alarm system, security alarm system, smart House. My solutions were created with MPU/MCU: PIC16F873, PIC18F452, PIC18F6621, PIC18F97j60, PIC32MX575F, ARM Cortex A7 and etc. Technologies I used: I2C, SPI, RS485, UART, Wiegand, 1-Wire, RFID, NFC, USB, Ethernet, Flash, ADC, EEPROM, and etc.
